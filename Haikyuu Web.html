About the Creator of Haikyuu

Haruichi Furudate

The creator of HaikyÅ« Haruichi Furudate, is a talented 41 year old manga artist and writer. He was born on March 7, 1983 in Japan, Iwate Prefecture and is employed currently in Shueisha Inc. As of now, Furudate has not publicly disclosed his gender identity, but the official Viz translation uses he/him to address him. He initially opted to continue playing volleyball professionally, but he had doubts that shifted his career path which led him to become a manga artist instead.
